Fun Facts about Abraham Lincoln:

  1. Wrestlemania!
    • He lost only one match out of around 300.
    • He was so respected that he is in the National Wrestling Hall of Fame.
  2. Top Hat: Fun and Functional!
    • He kept letters, notes, and important documents hidden in the crown of his tall hat.
    • He once was shot in the hat in a failed assassination attempt in 1864.
  3. Cats!
    • He loved cats! Lincoln once stopped a meeting to play with kittens.
    • He let a cat eat from the White House table, which irritated his wife.
